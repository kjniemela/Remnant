Taellor Allaxei

Age: 20
Gender: Non-binary
Race: Changeling
Element: None
Position: Renegade

Appearance: Their true form is a frail pale-skinned humanoid figure, with platinum blonde hair and white eyes. Typically appears as a gender-neutral wood elf with grey and light brown heterochromatic eyes - they think its interesting - and brown hair, typically around 5'8.

Personality: They are observational, determined, and patient. Pretty shy and sensitive to pain. Tends to be untrusting and non-confrontational. They sometimes feel like they don't actually have an identity. They tend to be afraid to show themselves for fear of being shunned. They are good at hiding, and experienced.

Backstory: He was born in Hilwahr to an elf father and a changeling mother [bit of a nasty shock for him when he found out.] His family also lives in Hilwahr. They have lived a relatively normal life, other than being shunned by some people.

Other: Always carries a pocket mirror. They are currently hiding in a small hamlet on the edge of Hilwahr, running a ship renting service. He flies people places in his ship.
