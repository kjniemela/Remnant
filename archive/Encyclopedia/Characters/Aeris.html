Aeris Wynzorwyn

Age: 28
Gender: Male
Race: Moon Elf
Element: Water
Position: Renegade

Appearance: Long white hair, braid on the side, no longer than back of his neck. Dressed in light blue +black garb, dark skin. Has a flask of bending water.

Personality: Soft and sweet, loud, gives advice to people of all ages, especially older than him. Quiet about his past, prefers to bury it under a humorous mask. Adamantly refuses to harm or kill another. friendly, talkative.

Backstory: Taken as a small child and trained in an underground program for waterbenders in Selasera. Brutal training, and he did not want to harm or kill the things he was forced to. He ran away on a ship he stole two years ago.
