Sorodath Havarin

Age: 167
Gender: Male
Race: Half Moon Elf, half Drow
Element: Water
Position: Renegade

Appearance: White, almost shoulder length hair. He his about 145 lbs. He has dark, dark blue skin, due to his Drow and Moon Elf heritage.
He was recently blinded. He has dark purple eyes with what is described as flecks of gold, usually obscured by a blindfold. He is about 6' 5".
Usually wears a black shirt, a black combat vest, and a black cloak. Typically has at least one of his three weapons on him. Generally inherited most of his fathers Drow traits, along with magical abilities but wasn't aware of their existence.

Personality: Prefers not to speak if the situation requires it. Tends to be untrusting. Calculating. Tries not to let emotions get in the way, but they sometimes do. Pretty forward thinking. Can be sarcastic if the situation calls upon it.

Relationships: Friends with most if not all of the renegades.
Has deep connections with the thieves guild and is one of it's more respected members. He has no family and grew up largely without them.
He's had many friends within the guild, most of whom have died.

Backstory: He was born in Uyalon but has been all over. He was born to a Moon Elf mother and a Drow father. His father longed to see the outside world and wanted to do something good with his life.
Unfortunately, his parents were supposedly killed when the rest of the village turned on his family because they were afraid of his father. He was a small child when they were killed and was sent to the streets, stealing, and begging until he found the thieves guild.
He worked for the thieves guild for many years. He has almost always been a loner with an exception of a few friends who have already passed on. He is experienced in thievery and assassination work.
