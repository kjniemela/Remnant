Vivenne "Vivi" Blackwood

Age: 16
Gender: Female
Race: Human
Element: Fire
Position: Renegade

Appearance: 5'5, slim and strong. Long brown hair in a ponytail, kinda drab clothing; locket around her neck, long sleeved blue shirt with a jacket, over the shoulder sack filled with general nick knacks she likes to tinker with, black boots.

Personality: Likes to tinker; intelligent and smart. Charismatic and likes to think two steps ahead if she can. Uses rage as a tool. Likes to get what she wants. competitive. Intense and calculating, but also very selfless. She likes to play games and build small tools and inventions in her spare time. She believes in the value of life, and will give second chances to those who she feels like deserves it.

Backstory: Had a rough upbringing, fought for her chance to be on the guildsmans ship to be able to get out of the shithole she was in.
