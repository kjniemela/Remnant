Owen Ulric Nerikas

Age: 17
Gender: Male
Race: Half-Elf
Element: None
Position: Renegade

Appearance: 6' 2" tall, strongly built. Curly white hair, blue eyes, tanned skin. Always looks shaggy because he just doesn't give a damn. Apparently smells severely of spices???

Personality: Reckless, never thinks before he speaks and often gets himself in trouble. Often far too candid. Never backs down. Intelligent, but prefers to make life fun rather than make it sensical.

Backstory: Ran away from his home town and joined a ship of bounty hunters, eventually ending up in the thieves guild.
