Annika of Tarati

Age: 13
Gender: Female
Race: Human
Element: Fire
Position: Renegade

Appearance: 4' 11", barely 70lbs. Long, raven black hair, amber eyes. Dressed in short, gray leggings and an old cloth tunic, as well as a tattered red vest she found God knows where. She used to have a black cloak but lost it in the fight with the Morgunstjarna.

Personality: Shy and quiet. Silently suffers through things rather than complaining. Emotional, but tries to bottle things up instead of taking about it. Sarcastic and spiteful once you get her started. Short temper. Intensely loyal to her friends.

Backstory: A child of the thieves guild. Her friends were killed in a skirmish between two rival bounty hunters.
