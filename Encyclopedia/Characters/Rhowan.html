Rhowan

Age: 63
Gender: Male
Race: Moon Elf
Element: None
Position: Bounty Hunter hired by the Morgunstjarna.

Appearance: Light blue skin, short dark blue hair, violet eyes. Short for an elf but still taller than most humans. Carries a bow and quiver of 2 dozen arrows. Wears dark brown and black. Wears dark mask.

Personality: Usually quiet but fairly friendly for an assassin/bounty hunter. Crafty and resourceful. He is observant and likes to figure out the people around him.

Backstory: He was born in Uyalon but has been all over. He didn't know his parents and was a beggar most of his childhood, which led his to a job as a bounty hunter and assassin.

Other: Along with his bow he has two short swords that he uses when he's out of arrows or in close combat - which he tries to avoid.
