Vargen Arnarson

Age: 17
Gender: Male
Race: Werewolf
Element: None
Position: Renegade

Appearance: Skinny and lanky, straight brown hair that goes to his neck, olive toned skin.

Personality: Chill and nonchalant. has seen it all and basically doesn't care. Is serious about fighting and keeping others safe and calm but provides mild comedic relief since he basically doesn't care about anything regarding an enemy. Loves food.

Backstory: Vargen left his cultist family bc he isn't very religious and they basically shunned him because of it. He travels around the world for the fun of it, finding different groups and leaving if they either end up dead or he loses them.
